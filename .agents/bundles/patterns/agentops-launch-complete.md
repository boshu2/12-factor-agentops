## üí° Core Positioning Principles

**Problem-First Always:**
1. Start with problem (agents failing in production)
2. Frame as hypothesis (can DevOps principles help?)
3. Position as explorer (I'm testing this)
4. Invite collaboration (help me validate)
5. Be honest about status (Alpha, seeking validation)

**Never:**
- Lead with credentials first
- Claim "I've solved this"
- Use metrics like "40x speedup" or "95% success"
- Say "proven" or "production-ready"
- Pitch as guru expert lecturing

**Always:**
- Say "I'm testing this hypothesis"
- Show failures AND successes
- Invite community to co-create
- Be transparent about what's experimental
- Welcome critical feedback
